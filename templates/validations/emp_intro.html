Welcome to One-Stop

The Westchester-Putnam One-Stop is a place that connects employers with prospective employees.

As a registered local employer you can access this site to connect with job-seekers in the Westchester/Putnam County area and be provided with access to their resumes and post jobs to recruit qualified candidates. We can help you respond to the demand for the skilled workers you are looking for, and identify trends that will inform, train, build and maintain a skilled workforce.

It's easy to get started. Simply create an account.
